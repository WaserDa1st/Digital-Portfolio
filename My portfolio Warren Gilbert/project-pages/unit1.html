Lastly when learning about online doucuments I learned about cloud computing and cloud stroage services such as Microsoft One Drive and Google drive and understood the benefits of using these services.
Threats to data and systems: Lastly I learned about the possible threats to data and systems this included knowing the differnce between Malware, phishing, formJacking, human error and trojans and understood how important cyber security is and how it involves implementing controls and safeguards to maintain the privacy, accuracy and accesbilty of digital infomation. These measures can cover a range of areas from protcting hardware components and physical spaces to securing networks and software systems. By ensuring that infomation is kept secure and free from unauthorized access, alteration or damage, computer security helps to safeguard agianst cyberthreats or breaches.
