About this course
In this unit I investigated the uses and features of databases by exploring what they are and what you can do with them, and how to apply my research and finding in my own database solutions
Topics Learnt / Skills that I have Developed

-The different types of databases

-Data types

-Tools and techniques

In unit 10 database the skills that I have developed is knowing the different types of databases such as flat and relational databases, local and networked and online databases. I then went on to understanding the different tools and techniques such as; table structure, field characteristics, indexing, relationships, forms and queries that build up and make a database. After I gained a good understanding of databases, I was given the task to follow instructions make a database about artists and albums with simple queries, reports and macros.
